Getting Started.

When you decide you want to buy or sell a large amount of digital assets over-the-counter, begin by approaching the First Bullion OTC desk and completing the onboarding documentation.

By clicking on the "Sign Up" button below. You will be redirected to our portal where you can open a line of communication with us.

Sign Up

Secure highest level of clearance.

To become an OTC client you must go through our onboarding process, obtaining the highest level of verification, to enable large transactions. All OTC clients are required to provide the following:

  • 1 Government ID

  • 2 Proof of Residence

  • 3 Proof of Income

Communicate with our desk member.

Open up a line of communication, via chat, text or voice call with the trade-desk about your preferred trade. It doesn’t have to be long, you can simply say:

You: “I would like to buy 500 Bitcoin.”

Our Trader: “I can sell you 500 BTC at a price of $8,000 USD per Bitcoin.”

Confirm the trade and wire funds.

You choose whether or not you wish to buy at the price offered. If you agree, the trade is confirmed, and the trader will ask for a deposit address where they can send the purchased assets.

After confirming the trade, wait for a confirmation of the transaction via email. In this email you’ll find wire instructions for the OTC bank account, where you will wire the funds.

Final Settlement

Once our OTC receives the wire, our trader will send the assets to the specified wallet address, along with a confirmation email to your inbox, and the trade is complete.
